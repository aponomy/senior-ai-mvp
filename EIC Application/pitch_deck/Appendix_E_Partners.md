## Partnership Value Beyond Revenue

### 1. Market Validation
- Real-world outcomes at scale (250-450 users minimum)
- Diverse user profiles (digital literacy, cognitive status, living situations)
- Cross-domain validation (banking, healthcare, transport, government)

### 2. De-Risking for Investors
- LOIs demonstrate demand before commercial launch
- Partner co-funding reduces burn (pilot incentives from partners)
- Validation of B2B2C model (70% of partnerships in this channel)

### 3. Distribution Leverage
- Partners bring users at low CAC (â‚¬40-60 vs. â‚¬120-160 B2C)
- Built-in trust (municipal/payer endorsement)
- Onboarding support (partner staff assist)

### 4. Regulatory Compliance
- Accessibility Act: Partners as test sites for EN 301 549
- AI Act: Real-world risk management and monitoring
- GDPR: Data processing agreements with institutional partners

### 5. Competitive Moat
- 3-5 year contracts create switching costs
- Partner integrations (care systems, payer platforms, pharmacy APIs)
- "European elderly AI" brand through trusted institutions
