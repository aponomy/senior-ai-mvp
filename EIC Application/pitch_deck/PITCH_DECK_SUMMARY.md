## üìê Design Specifications (Recommendations)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Large, bold sans-serif (32-48pt)
- **Body text**: 18-24pt minimum (readability for screen and print)
- **Captions**: 14-16pt minimum

### Colors
- **Primary**: Trust-building blues (#0066CC, #4A90E2)
- **Secondary**: Soft greens (#66BB6A, #81C784) for growth/health
- **Accent**: Warm orange (#FF9800) sparingly for emphasis
- **Background**: White or very light gray (#F5F5F5)
- **Text**: Dark gray (#212121) for contrast, not pure black
- **EU colors**: Blue + yellow/gold for EU identity moments

### Layout
- **One key message per slide** - no crowding
- **Visual hierarchy**: Big ‚Üí small (most important first)
- **White space**: Generous margins, breathing room
- **Icons**: Simple, recognizable (avoid complex illustrations)
- **Charts**: Clean, labeled, color-blind-friendly

### Accessibility
- **Contrast ratio**: 4.5:1 minimum (WCAG AA)
- **Color**: Never rely on color alone (use patterns, icons, labels)
- **Alt text**: For all images (if presenting digitally)
- **Fonts**: Sans-serif, no script or decorative fonts

## üé¨ Delivery Tips (From Expert Consultation)

### Script Preparation
- **1-2 sentences per slide maximum**
- **10 minutes total** (14 slides = ~40 seconds/slide average, some longer/shorter)
- **Memorize key transitions** so flow is smooth
- **End with vision** - emotional, aspirational close

### Body Language
- **Confidence**: Stand, make eye contact, project energy
- **Passion**: Let belief in mission show through
- **Clarity**: Speak slowly enough for non-native English speakers
- **Accessibility**: Consider live captions if presenting remotely

### Q&A Preparation
- **Appendix slides ready** - toggle to them quickly
- **Anticipate questions**:
  - Technical: How does Conversation Atlas actually work?
  - Market: How do you compete with Big Tech?
  - Financial: When do you break even? What if growth is slower?
  - Risk: What if AI Act delays? What if users don't adopt?
  - Team: Why are you the right team? Who else do you need?
- **Honesty**: Don't BS - say "I don't know, but here's how we'd find out"
