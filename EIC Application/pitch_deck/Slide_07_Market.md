# Elderly Digital Assistance Market

---

## Market Sizing (5-year horizon, 2025-2030)

**TAM** (Total Addressable Market): **€12.7B**  
EU elderly digital assistance (70-85M users × €150-180 ARPU)

**SAM** (Serviceable Available Market): **€6.8B**  
Tier 1-2 metro + urban areas (tech-accessible infrastructure)

**SOM** (Serviceable Obtainable Market, Y5): **€380-410M**  
2.0-2.3M users in beachhead segments × €167-179 ARPU

---

## Beachhead Strategy (Years 1-2)

### **Scandinavia + DACH** (Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Germany, Austria)
- BankID/eIDAS integration mature
- Healthcare/ePrescription infrastructure ready
- High digital literacy (65-70% elderly online)
- Willingness to pay (€150-180/year)

### **Domains** (4 → 14 over 18 months)
**Phase 1:** Healthcare, Banking, Transport, Government  
**Phase 2:** Shopping, Food delivery, Energy, Tax  
**Phase 3:** Legal, Insurance, Entertainment, Communication, Travel, Home services
