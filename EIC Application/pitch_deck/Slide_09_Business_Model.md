# Revenue Streams

---

## Three-Tier Model

### **B2C Direct** (40% of revenue)
€9.99/month base + €3-5 domain packs  
→ €120-180 ARPU annually

### **B2B2C White-Label** (45% of revenue)
Banks, insurers, municipalities subsidize  
→ €30-50 ARPU (volume pricing)

### **B2B Enterprise** (15% of revenue)
Call center automation, care homes  
→ €600-1,200/seat/year

---

## Unit Economics (Year 3 Steady State)

### **LTV** (Lifetime Value): **€480-540**
3-4 years average retention × €120-180 ARPU  
85-90% gross margin

### **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost): **€140-160**
Performance marketing + B2B2C partnerships

### **LTV:CAC Ratio**: **3.2:1** (Year 3) → **5.1:1** (Year 5)
Improved by network effects + organic referrals

### **Payback Period**: **11-13 months**
