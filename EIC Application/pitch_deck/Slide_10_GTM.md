# Multi-Channel GTM (18-month rollout)

---

## Five Distribution Channels

### 1. **B2C Direct** (Self-serve SaaS)
Performance marketing + content (Google, Meta, YouTube)  
Target: Tech-literate elderly (68-78 years)  
CAC: €80-120

### 2. **B2B2C White-Label** (Banks, Insurers)
Co-branded digital assistance  
12 pilots secured (Nordea, Folksam, Allianz exploring)  
Volume pricing: €30-50 ARPU

### 3. **Municipal Partnerships** (Digital Inclusion Programs)
EU Digital Decade funding  
Subsidized access for low-income elderly  
Pilot: 3 Swedish municipalities (Malmö, Uppsala, Linköping)

### 4. **NGO/Senior Centers** (Community Distribution)
Workshops, demos, peer referrals  
40+ senior centers in pipeline (Scandinavia + DACH)

### 5. **Pharmacy/Healthcare Chains** (Point-of-Need)
In-store kiosks, prescription pickup demos  
Apoteket (Sweden), dm-drogerie (Germany) in discussions

---

**150-250 users** (Beta, 8 domains)
