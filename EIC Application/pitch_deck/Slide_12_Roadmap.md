# Development Timeline

---

## Three-Phase Rollout

### **Phase 1: Alpha** (M1-M6)
**Deliverables:**
- 4 domains live (Healthcare, Banking, Transport, Government)
- 50-100 test users (Sweden)
- BankID, FHIR, transport API integrations
- TRL 6 validation

**Key Milestones:**
- M3: First domain integration (Healthcare ePrescription)
- M6: Alpha launch, AI Act risk management documented

---

### **Phase 2: Beta** (M7-M12)
**Deliverables:**
- 8 domains (add Shopping, Food, Energy, Tax)
- 150-250 users (Scandinavia)
- B2B2C pilots (3 banks, 2 municipalities)
- TRL 7 validation

**Key Milestones:**
- M9: EN 301 549 accessibility audit
- M12: Production v1.0 ready, eIDAS integration pilot

---

### **Phase 3: Production** (M13-M18)
**Deliverables:**
- 14 domains (add Legal, Insurance, Entertainment, Communication, Travel, Home services)
- 250-450 users (Scandinavia + DACH entry)
- All 5 GTM channels active
- TRL 8 complete

**Key Milestones:**
- M15: ISO 27001 certification
- M18: Series A ready (€6.5-7M ask)

---
