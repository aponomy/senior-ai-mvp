# EIC Accelerator Application Form (Part B)

> **Stage 2 - Full Application**

---

## Important Information

### Page Limit

- **Maximum:** 20 pages (including cover page)
- Excess pages will be automatically made invisible to evaluators
- You can remove instructions and guidance before submission
- Keep the section headings

### Formatting Requirements

- **Font:** Times New Roman (or equivalent)
- **Size:** Minimum 11 points
- **Spacing:** Minimum single line spacing
- **Page size:** A4
- **Margins:** Minimum 15mm (all sides)

### Use of Generative AI Tools

When using AI tools for proposal preparation, you must:

1. **Verify** accuracy, validity, and appropriateness of AI-generated content
2. **Provide** a list of sources used to generate content
3. **Check** citations for accuracy
4. **Be conscious** of potential plagiarism
5. **Acknowledge** AI tool limitations (bias, errors, gaps)
6. **Disclose** which AI tools were used and how
