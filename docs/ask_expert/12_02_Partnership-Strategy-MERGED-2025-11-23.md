---

## Sales Cycles: Cold Outreach → Signed Pilot

### Swedish Municipalities (Digital Inclusion, Welfare Tech)
- **Typical**: 4–8 months
- **Fast path** (direct award, strong champion): 3–4 months
- **Slow path** (committee approvals): 9–12 months
- **Notes**: Can sign small pilots under direct-procurement thresholds. Decisions pause June–August and mid-Dec–mid-Jan.

### Norwegian Municipalities (Velferdsteknologi)
- **Typical**: 3–6 months
- **Fast path**: 2–3 months if running welfare tech pilots with LUP procedures
- **Slow path**: 7–9 months if legal/IT escalations

### Private Care Providers
- **Typical**: 2–4 months
- **Fast path**: 4–8 weeks if site manager + regional ops co-sponsor
- **Slow path**: 5–6 months if head office procurement involved

### Health Payers
- **Typical**: 6–12 months
- **Fast path** (CSR/value-add pilot): 4–6 months
- **Slow path** (clinical/claims ROI scrutiny): 12–18 months

### Telcos
- **Typical**: 4–8 months
- **Fast path** (CSR/brand led): 3–4 months
- **Slow path** (device/billing integration): 9–12 months

---

## Strategy for 10-15 Pilots in 18 Months

- **6–8 municipal pilots** started months 6–12
- **4–5 private care pilots** started months 3–9
- **1–2 telco/insurer pilots** started months 9–15

**Approach**: Work fast-cycle segments (care, forward municipalities) first.

# B2B2C Partnership: Pilot Pricing & Structure

**Date**: 2025-11-23  
**Issue**: #12 - Implementation Plan & Team  
**Task**: 02 - Partnership Strategy  
**Type**: Expert Consultation - Part 6 of 6

---

## Pilot Structure

- **Duration**: 8–12 weeks
- **Participants**: 20–40
- **Staff**: 1–2 champions
- **IT**: Light footprint

---

## Pricing Options

### 1. Paid Pilot (Recommended)
- **300–600 SEK** per participant (full pilot)
- OR **100–200 SEK/user/month**
- Includes onboarding + debrief
- Signals value, reduces churn

### 2. Co-Funded Pilot
- Municipal innovation funds (SKR/Vinnova/LUP) + in-kind
- Small fixed fee + success fee on conversion

### 3. Free Pilot (Limited)
- Only if: marquee logo, multi-site commitment, co-branded PR
- Keep short: 6–8 weeks, 20–25 users max

---

## Conversion Incentives

- Pre-agree price book: €6–9 municipal, €6–8 care
- "Pilot-to-production" discount if signed within 60 days
- Device loaners + MDM/kiosk mode

---

## Success Metrics

### Quantitative
- Staff time reduction: 50–80% target
- Task completion rates
- Weekly active rate >60%

### Qualitative
- Self-efficacy improvement (eHEALS)
- SUS >70, NPS >30
- Caregiver feedback

# B2B2C Partnership: Procurement Requirements

**Date**: 2025-11-23  
**Issue**: #12 - Implementation Plan & Team  
**Task**: 02 - Partnership Strategy  
**Type**: Expert Consultation - Part 2 of 6

---

## Decision Criteria & Procurement Requirements

Must satisfy BEFORE first meetings:

---

## Legal/GDPR Requirements

### Data Processing Agreements
- DPA (biträdesavtal) template ready
- You as processor, clear controller roles
- Data residency in EU/EEA
- **Critical**: Avoid US-controlled processors (Cloud Act/Schrems II)
- Offer EU-owned cloud option (Cleura/OVHcloud)

### Privacy Documentation
- DPIA template
- Records of Processing
- Sub-processor list
- Encryption in transit/at rest
- Access controls, audit logging
- Retention/deletion policy
- Incident response playbook
- BankID support

---

## Information Security

- ISMS aligned to ISO 27001
- Penetration test or security review
- **Sweden**: KLASSA classification, MSB guidance
- TIA (Transfer Impact Assessment) if cross-border

---

## Accessibility/Usability

- WCAG 2.1 AA conformance
- EN 301 549 self-declaration
- Evidence of testing with 65+ users
- Large text, high-contrast, voice, plain language
- **Norway**: Universal Design regulations, UU-tilsynet compliance

---

## Clinical/Regulatory

- Non-medical scope (avoid MDR classification)
- Informational health advice only
- Clear disclaimers
- Escalation to human services
- No diagnosis/treatment

---

## Procurement Mechanics

- Time-boxed, capped-value pilots under direct-procurement thresholds
- Simple SOW: objectives, duration (8-12 weeks), cohort (20-40), metrics, data plan, price
- Public Sector Terms: termination for convenience, data return, no lock-in
