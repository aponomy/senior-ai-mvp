## What Changed

Added **"accessibility layer for the entire AI ecosystem"** positioning to Senior AI materials. This emphasizes that we make thousands of existing AI services (ChatGPT, Midjourney, DALL-E, Claude, etc.) accessible to elderly users through our **Create/Learn/Connect/Execute** framework, wrapped in our unique **Conversation Atlas** interface.

**Key Principles**:
- **Scope**: Addition, not replacement - BOTH perspectives maintained
- **Balance**: Equal weight to creative + utility, slight utility emphasis
- **AI Services**: Leading models (not repackaging like Firefly/Perplexity)
- **Differentiation**: (a) Elderly accessibility + (b) Conversation Atlas technology
- **Framing**: Empowerment ("join AI revolution") over defensive ("don't get left behind")

- **Surgical edits**: Targeted key sections (executive summaries, problem statements, competitive positioning) for maximum impact
- **Consistency framework**: Create/Learn/Connect/Execute provided clear structure to add everywhere
